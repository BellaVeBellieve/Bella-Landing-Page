About Isabel

Isabel VE

Leadership Coach · Emotional Wisdom Specialist · EMCC Accredited Supervisor

I am a systemic coach who blends emotional wisdom, somatic awareness, and design thinking to help people lead with empathy and clarity.

Over 20 years and across 11 cities in 7 countries, I have guided professionals through life's most demanding transitions. I work at the intersection of the nervous system, emotional intelligence, and the body's own wisdom — because real change lives in the body before it reaches the mind.

With a background in systems engineering, behavioural science, and cultural dynamics, I guide leaders and individuals towards meaningful, lasting transformation.
