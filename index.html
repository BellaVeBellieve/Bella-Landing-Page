Your Personal Blueprint

Most people come to coaching with something specific on their mind — a relationship that is not working, a career transition, a body that will not rest, a version of themselves they have not yet become. Over four sessions, we go beneath the surface. We find the patterns, the beliefs, the emotional signals you have been trying to ignore. About Isabel

Isabel Vallejo Echavarria

Leadership Coach & Emotional Wisdom Specialist

I am a systemic coach who blends emotional wisdom, somatic awareness, and design thinking to help people lead with empathy and clarity. Born in Colombia, I have spent 20 years guiding professionals through life's most demanding transitions across 11 cities in 7 countries. I work at the intersection of the nervous system, emotional intelligence, and the body's own wisdom — because real change lives in the body before it reaches the mind.
