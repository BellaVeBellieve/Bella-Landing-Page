What supervision is

Where you bring the work that stays with you.

The session that didn't land. The client you can't quite read. The moment you wondered whether it was them — or you.

Supervision is a space to explore without preconceptions. To try things out, to get it wrong and understand why. To ask the questions you haven't yet put into words.

After a session you feel more clearly yourself. You've had a space to hear yourself think, reflect, reflex, and start over. With a wider view of who you are as a coach, and of the clients you serve.

Nurtured by coaching, mentoring, and more. The fertile ground from which everything else in your practice can grow.
